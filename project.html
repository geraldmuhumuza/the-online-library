BACKGROUND

The library was started in 2017 with thw aim of increasing the reading nature of the people mostly in Uganda.
The online library will make sure the reading materials are brought nearer to the individuals who need them.

MISSION

To increase the people's reading capability in their daily life through providing them the necessary
reading material in their comfort zone.

To enhance the research activity among the people most especially students. This comes with the saying
that if one wants to give something to the people put it on internet which will make it available for
every person in need of it for use.

To make the learning and teaching material available to every person despite the location and time when
the material is needed

ABOUT

The online library is a fast growing library with over 100million viewers and started in 2017.
It provides both science and literature books for someone to easily do the research, leisure, science
and arts education and historical books. It also provides people with recent reports and education and
some references when needed.
